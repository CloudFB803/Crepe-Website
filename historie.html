History of the Crepe

The crepe has a rich history that dates back to the 13th century in France.

Originally made from buckwheat flour, crepes were a nutritious meal for the poor.

Over the years, they evolved into a popular dish throughout Europe and the world.

Today, both sweet and savory crepes are enjoyed by people of all ages.

Whether filled with fruits, chocolate, or cheese, crepes offer a delightful dining experience.
